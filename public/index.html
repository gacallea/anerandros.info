About

Eclectic non-musician and producer of cinematic ambient, avant-garde, left-field, and softronica. Fond of improvisation, of the studio as an instrument and of meticulously prepared live shows; while preserving empathy and intuition as factors when drawing oneiric soundscapes into a story, in the making of musical voyages. VFS Alumni and Sound Design for Visual Media graduate.

Member of Mi.S.Fu and Sleep Collective: a multifunctional, versatile cross-media laboratory dedicated to mapping, music, 3D, arts; and a collective consisting of talents that had given rise to various Sleep Concert in different locations and with different line-ups. Formerly based in Bristol, UK, Aner Andros has performed at the Cube Cinema Plex and have collaborated with Parallel Madness. Also noteworthy are the live shows at Crux Events in London and the Cronosfera Festival in Italy.
